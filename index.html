01 / ABOUT US

About Us

PT. BHUWANA INDO MARSHADA is a company specializing in integrated logistics and transportation services, headquartered in Batam, Riau Islands, Indonesia. We are committed to becoming a strategic partner in supporting the seamless movement of goods across international and domestic markets through professional, reliable, and customer-focused services.
We provide comprehensive logistics solutions, including International Sea & Air Freight Forwarding, ensuring efficient and secure cargo transportation worldwide. In addition, we offer complete and accurate Customs Clearance & PPJK (Indonesian Customs Brokerage) services, assisting clients with all export and import documentation and regulatory requirements to ensure smooth customs processing.
